CyberArk

CyberArk announces agentic identity capabilities in Identity Security Platform

PAM-first approach to agent lifecycle management. Interesting but narrow — locking down privileged access isn't the same as orchestrating dynamic delegation. Watch for how they handle non-privileged agent flows.

Mar 2026
Ping Identity

Ping ships MCP Gateway preview in PingOne platform

Gateway interceptor model for MCP traffic. Smart strategy — inspect and authorize at the gateway layer. Question is whether they can execute at the speed the agentic ecosystem is moving.

Feb 2026
Dscope

Dscope raises $12M Series A for non-human identity management

NHI startup focused on secret sprawl and service account governance. Solid niche, but the real opportunity is in agent identity — which requires more than credential management.

Feb 2026
IETF

OAuth 2.1 draft advances with PKCE requirement and implicit flow removal

Long overdue cleanup. PKCE as mandatory and killing implicit flow are the right calls. Now we need the working groups to tackle agent-specific grant types.
