Give Your Users a Diamond Status Authentication Experience

Identiverse 2024 — Las Vegas • May 29, 2024
Using the metaphor of airline Diamond Status to rethink authentication UX. Today's users endure multiple logins across OS, browser, email, and VPN — the equivalent of standing in every airport line. This talk makes the case for a "Diamond Status" experience: secure passwordless desktop login with trusted sessions persisting across all applications, plus dynamic post-authentication risk assessment that reacts to threats without frustrating users.
